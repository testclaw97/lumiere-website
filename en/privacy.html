2. Hosting

This website is hosted on our own server. When you visit the website, technical data (so-called server log files) is automatically collected, including:

  • IP address of the accessing device
  • Date and time of access
  • Page visited / file name
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system used
  • Referrer URL

This data is collected to ensure the smooth operation of the website and to improve our services. The legal basis for this is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest).

3. Contact Form

If you send us enquiries via the contact form, your details from the enquiry form, including the contact information you provide (name, email address, practice, phone, message), will be stored by us for the purpose of processing the enquiry and in case of follow-up questions.

The processing of this data is based on Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual measures) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in processing your enquiry).

To transmit the form data, we use the service Formspree (Formspree, Inc., USA). Formspree processes the data you enter for the purpose of forwarding it to us. For more information, please refer to Formspree's privacy policy.

4. External Services

Fonts

This website uses the "Inter" font, which is hosted locally on our server. When loading the fonts, no connection to external servers (e.g., Google) is established. Your IP address is not transmitted to third parties.

Tailwind CSS (CDN)

We use the Tailwind CSS framework via a Content Delivery Network (CDN). When loading the page, a connection to the CDN provider's servers is established. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

5. Your Rights

You have the right at any time to:

  • Access information about your personal data stored by us (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Erasure of your data (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection supervisory authority.

6. Cookies

This website does not use its own cookies. However, the integration of external services (Google Fonts, Tailwind CDN) may result in third-party cookies being set.

7. SSL/TLS Encryption

For security reasons, this website uses SSL or TLS encryption. You can recognize an encrypted connection by the browser's address bar changing from "http://" to "https://". When SSL or TLS encryption is activated, the data you transmit to us cannot be read by third parties.
