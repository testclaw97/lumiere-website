Frequently Asked Questions

How much does social media marketing cost for my practice?
Our packages start at €500/month for the essentials. Depending on scope and platforms, costs range from €500 to €3,000 per month. During your free audit, we'll discuss which package best fits your practice.
How quickly will I see results?
You'll typically see first visible improvements (more reach, engagement) within 30 days. For sustainable patient acquisition, we recommend planning for 2-3 months of continuous work.
Do I need to create content myself?
No. We handle all content creation — from copy and graphics to videos. Occasionally we'll ask for quick photos or clips from your daily practice, which we then professionally edit and produce.
Which platforms are best for my practice?
For most dental practices and cosmetic studios, we recommend Instagram and TikTok as primary channels. Instagram for trust and professionalism, TikTok for reaching younger audiences. During your audit, we'll analyze where your target audience is most active.
Is there a minimum contract period?
We recommend a collaboration of at least 3 months so the strategy can reach its full potential. However, there's no rigid lock-in — we convince through results, not contracts.
What sets Lumiere apart from other agencies?
We're not a generic agency. We work exclusively with dental practices and cosmetic studios — meaning we know your industry, your audience, and what works. On top of that, we use AI-powered tools that make our work faster and more precise.
