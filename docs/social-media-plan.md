# Lumiere Media & Marketing — 90-Day Social Media Plan

**Purpose:** This is Lumiere's own social media strategy. When a dentist receives cold outreach and checks our Instagram, they must see an active, professional, knowledgeable agency. The feed IS the portfolio.

**Channels:** Instagram (primary), LinkedIn (B2B credibility), TikTok (reach & virality)

---

## Content Pillars

Rotate these 4 themes. Every post maps to exactly one pillar.

| # | Pillar | What it covers | Why it works |
|---|--------|---------------|--------------|
| 1 | **Praxis-Tipps** (Practice Tips) | Actionable social media advice for dental/cosmetic practices — what to post, how to photograph treatments, caption formulas | Positions Lumiere as the expert. Dentists save these posts. |
| 2 | **KI im Einsatz** (AI in Action) | Behind-the-scenes of how Lumiere uses AI tools — content generation, analytics, scheduling, image editing | Differentiator. No other dental marketing agency in Germany shows this. |
| 3 | **Ergebnisse** (Results) | Before/after case studies, metrics, client wins, ROI breakdowns | Social proof. The only thing that truly sells. |
| 4 | **Branche & Trends** (Industry & Trends) | Dental marketing trends, algorithm updates, what top practices worldwide are doing on social | Shows Lumiere stays current. Builds authority. |

**Ratio per week:** 2x Praxis-Tipps, 1x KI im Einsatz, 1x Ergebnisse, 1x Branche & Trends = 5 posts/week

## Growth Tactics

### Daily Engagement (15 min/day)

1. **Comment on 10 dental practice posts** — meaningful comments, not generic. Example: "Tolles Vorher/Nachher! Welches Komposit habt ihr verwendet?" (shows you understand dentistry)
2. **Reply to every comment and DM within 2 hours**
3. **Like and save competitor/peer content** (signals to algorithm)
4. **Share relevant dental posts to Stories** with added commentary

### Weekly Outreach

1. **Follow 20 dental practices** in target cities (Munich, Hamburg, Berlin, Cologne, Frankfurt, Dusseldorf)
2. **DM 5 practices** with genuine compliment + soft pitch (see Legitimacy Playbook for script)
3. **Engage with dental supplier accounts** (Ivoclar, Straumann, Henry Schein) — their followers are your target audience

### Monthly Collaborations

1. **Pitch one Instagram Live** with a dentist or dental supplier
2. **Guest post** on a dental blog or LinkedIn newsletter
3. **Cross-promote** with a complementary service provider (dental photographer, practice management consultant)

### Content Amplification

1. **Repurpose every post:** Instagram carousel → LinkedIn post → TikTok video → Story sequence → Newsletter snippet
2. **Pin top 3 posts** on Instagram (best case study, best tip carousel, intro post)
3. **Use Instagram's Collab feature** when posting about a client (their followers see it too)
4. **Boost 1 post/month** with €20-50 ad spend targeting dentists in Germany (Instagram allows job title targeting)

### Tracking Tools

- **Instagram Insights** (built-in) — reach, engagement, follower demographics
- **LinkedIn Analytics** (built-in) — impressions, profile views, connection requests
- **TikTok Analytics** (built-in) — views, completion rate, shares
- **Google Analytics** — track social → website traffic with UTM parameters
- **Simple spreadsheet** — log weekly numbers every Sunday, spot trends

### Review Cadence

- **Weekly (Sunday):** 15-min check of all metrics, adjust next week's content if something worked/flopped
- **Monthly (1st of month):** Full review, update strategy, set next month's targets
- **Quarterly (after 90 days):** Major strategy review — what worked, what didn't, double down or pivot
